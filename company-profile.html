COMPANY POLICY

We will not alow discrimination on grounds of religion, race or color. We will not allow employment of anyone under the age of eighteen years. We will Provided childcare facility during working hours. We will provide reasonaable medical facility and subsidized food for our employees. we will compensate employees on a fair and timely manner for servies provided to the company.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Quality Assurance covers all activities from design, development, production, installation, servicing and documentation, this introduced the rule “Fit for purpose” and “do it right first time”. We got TQM expertise that put their every effort to maintain the quality of raw materials, assemblies, products and components; Services related to production; and management, production, and inspection Processes, which means “Quality Awareness” throughout the company.

OUR ENVIRONMENT

At “M U Fashion Ltd”, sustainable and responsible business has always been a top priority, which is expressed through our sense of responsibility towards the community and the environment. Hence, corporate social responsibility (CSR) has always been an integral part of our corporate framework. Our CSR activities broadly take the forms of environmental sustainability, social responsibility and cultural involvement.

PROVIDING EXCELLENCE

We believe you will try with us, by providing the opportunity to serve; you will access hassle free, quality oriented, cost effective garments to your doorstep. We have earned a reputation in the global apparel industry as one of the foremost factory in Bangladesh for our commitment to quality & timely delivery. Through our extensive sourcing network, we have the ability to effectively procure the best materials. Our customers rely on us to deliver the best quality products and superb service.
